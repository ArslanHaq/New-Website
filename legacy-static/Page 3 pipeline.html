Lead Candidate: PRX-474 Inhibits miR-221

Why is miR-221 a prime therapeutic target?

Broad therapeutic potential across multiple tumor types — covering 75-80% of all cancer indications.

First-in-class opportunity

Represents a first-in-class therapeutic opportunity to directly target miR-221 in oncology.

Widely overexpressed

Overexpressed across multiple cancer types including hematological malignancies and solid tumors.

Suppresses tumor suppressors

Directly suppresses CDKN1B/p27 (cell cycle control) and PTEN (tumor suppression).

Strong preclinical evidence

Inhibiting miR-221 using PRX-474 leads to impressive anti-tumor activity and upregulation of tumor suppressor genes.
